FALL STUDENT SHOWCASE
Salisbury University

Co-Lighting Designer

This was my first time doing lighting for dance pieces, and was a fun challenge to learn how movement in dance was affected by lighting. I worked with student choreographers to design lighting for three of the dance pieces during this show.
Director: Helen Myers

Lighting Designers: John Raley and James Benedict

Choreographers: Emmi Layton, Emma Seichepine, Maura Gorman, Paige Houseal, Kaci Willey, Amani Howard, Danielle Malley
