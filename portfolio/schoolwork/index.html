FUNDAMENTALS OF THEATRE
Salisbury University

THEA-210
This is some work I did for the fundamentals of theatre course. This is a set design for antigone featuring working light up lanterns and fireplace and a lighting design project that challenged me to design without color.


COSTUMING AND THEATRE CRAFTS
Salisbury University

THEA-126
This is the final costume design I created for the costuming and theatre crafts course I took at Salisbury University. The costume I created is an abstract representation of the French poet Charles Baudelaire, who wrote about themes of light contrasting with darkness. The costume took about a month to make altogether.
