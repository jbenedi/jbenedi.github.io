CAPSTONE SENIOR STAGECRAFT
Salisbury University

Lighting/Sound/Projections, Technical Director

Final presentation for the university’s senior capstone course, consisting of four individual one-acts, produced and put together from start to finish by the senior class. Pieces were selected, planned, rehearsed, teched, and produced over the span of one semester. Pieces: Breath (Beckett), Come and Go (Beckett), The Bear (Checkov), The Proposal (Checkov)
Directors: Grey Reynolds, Lauryn Damron, AJ Fontaine

Lighting Designers: James Benedict, Grey Reynolds, Conner Steffy

Scenic Designers: Riley Webster, Caroline Lawson, Sierra Wakefield

Sound Designers: James Benedict, Conner Steffy

Costume Designers: Matt Bannister, Caroline Lawson, Sierra Wakefield
