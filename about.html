Hi, I'm James!


I'm a university student currently studying Theatre Design and Production with a focus in lighting at Salisbury University! I have been learning about and studying theatrical lighting since my freshmen year of high school.

I currently have 17 productions of experience under my belt (primarily in lighting) divided between high school, local theatre, and my university.

My goal after university is to become a lighting designer for mainly theatrical productions, but musical and concert type lighting design interests me as well.

Some other interests I have are photography and coding. You can see some of my photography in the photography tab above, and as for my coding, I coded this website from scratch without the help of any website builders!
