Hi, I'm James!


I'm a lighting designer and MFA candidate at Wayne State University with over seven years of experience across more than 40 productions. My work spans youth theatre, university productions, regional opera, and more, with expertise in design, programming, and electrics, including intelligent fixtures, projections, and media playback systems.

Every choice I make in a design is intentional. I treat darkness as a design element as much as light itself, using contrast, shadow, and negative space to shape how an audience experiences a story. I'm focused on consistency throughout a production, developing a visual language for each show so that every moment feels like it belongs to the same world. My primary focus is theatrical lighting, with a growing interest in dance and live events.

Outside of lighting, I work in photography, exploring how color and shadow convey emotion, a practice that directly informs how I think about light on stage. I also code, and built this website from scratch.
